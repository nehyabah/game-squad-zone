# Quick Reference: NFL Spread Betting Guide

## What is a Spread?

A **point spread** levels the playing field between a favorite and underdog by giving/taking points.

---

## Spread Notation

### Negative Spread (-)
**Means: FAVORITE** - Team must win by MORE than this number

Examples:
- **Chiefs -7.5**: Chiefs must win by 8+ points
- **Bills -3.5**: Bills must win by 4+ points
- **Ravens -10.5**: Ravens must win by 11+ points

### Positive Spread (+)
**Means: UNDERDOG** - Team can lose by UP TO this number and still cover

Examples:
- **Bengals +7.5**: Bengals can lose by 7 or less (or win)
- **Patriots +3.5**: Patriots can lose by 3 or less (or win)
- **Panthers +10.5**: Panthers can lose by 10 or less (or win)

## Leaderboard Rankings

### How Rankings Work
1. **Win Percentage** (Primary)
   - Formula: `(Wins + Pushes/2) / Total Games √ó 100`
   - Pushes count as 0.5 wins

2. **Total Points** (Tie-Breaker)
   - If two users have same win %, higher points ranks first

### Example Standings
```
Rank | Player | Record    | Win %  | Points
-----|--------|-----------|--------|-------
1    | Alice  | 8-2-0     | 80.0%  | 80
2    | Bob    | 7-2-1     | 75.0%  | 70
3    | Carol  | 6-3-1     | 65.0%  | 60
```

**Why Bob ranks #2:**
- Bob: (7 + 1/2) / 10 = 75%
- Carol: (6 + 1/2) / 10 = 65%

## Glossary

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Spread** | Points added/subtracted from team's score |
| **Cover** | Beat the spread (win your bet) |
| **Favorite** | Team expected to win (negative spread) |
| **Underdog** | Team expected to lose (positive spread) |
| **Push** | Tie after spread applied (rare) |
| **Pick 'Em** | No spread (0.0), just pick winner |
| **Line** | Another term for spread |
| **Lock** | A pick you're confident will win |

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

‚ùå **Confusing spread with expected score**
- Spread is about margin of victory, not final score

‚ùå **Forgetting negative spreads mean winning by MORE**
- Chiefs -7.5 means they must win by 8+, not 7

‚ùå **Thinking underdogs must win**
- Underdogs can cover by losing within the spread

‚ùå **Picking all favorites**
- Favorites don't always cover; diversify your picks

‚ùå **Ignoring push possibility**
- Whole number spreads (7.0, 3.0) can push

## Quick Math Check

### To Check If Your Pick Won:
1. Find your team's actual score
2. Add the spread to it
3. Compare to opponent's score
4. If higher ‚Üí You Win! ‚úÖ
5. If lower ‚Üí You Lose ‚ùå
6. If equal ‚Üí Push üîÑ

### Example Calculation:
```
You picked: Patriots +3.5
Final: Bills 20, Patriots 17

Step 1: Patriots scored 17
Step 2: 17 + 3.5 = 20.5
Step 3: 20.5 vs Bills' 20
Step 4: 20.5 > 20 ‚Üí You WIN! ‚úÖ
```
